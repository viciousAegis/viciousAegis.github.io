Akshit Sinha

I am an incoming M.Phil student at the University of Cambridge in the Machine Learning and Machine Intelligence (MLMI) program. Read below to learn more about my research interests and past work.

Before this, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering with Honors from IIIT Hyderabad. As an undergraduate, I worked on machine learning with graphs under the supervision of Ponnurangam Kumaraguru at the amazing Precog lab.

Email  /  GitHub  /  Google Scholar  /  Twitter  /  LinkedIn

My current research focuses on understanding, evaluating and improving capabilities of AI systems (mainly large language models). My eventual goal is to help build AI that can reliably and autonomously perform complex tasks for extremely long periods of time, without human intervention.
During my undergraduate studies, I worked on problems in interpretability and machine unlearning, with a focus on graph neural networks.
