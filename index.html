hello, i'm currently a senior undergraduate student at iiit hyderabad pursuing a bachelor's degree with honors in computer science. i am a researcher at the precog research group advised by dr. ponnurangam kumaraguru. my interests lie broadly in understanding neural networks operating on graph data. more specifically, i am interested in the following topics:

  • self-supervised learning on graphs
  • interpretability in graph neural networks
  • trustworthy machine learning
  • neural algorithmic properties of graph-based networks
  • foundational models for graph learning

besides the technical stuff, i run a (slightly inactive) youtube channel, i love watching football (liverpool fc; ynwa), playing video games, watching anime, playing chess, listening to music (mostly indie rock/pop), and occasionally reading sci-fi/dystopian/philosophical fiction specifically from 1960-1970s.
